About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Binghamton University (SUNY Binghamton), The State University of New York, advised by Prof. Ping Yang. I work on AI-based Security, Trustworthy AI, Malware Detection, and Robot Security.

I received my M.S. in Computer and Information Science from Cleveland State University (2016-2018), and my M.S. and B.S. degrees in Control Science and Automation from Harbin Institute of Technology (2006-2008, 2002-2006).
